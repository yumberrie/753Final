Paintings andd Drawings

Having majored in studio art at University of California at Santa Barbara, Liz is a lifelong artist. Her primary medium is acryrlic on canvas, but she has also painted on wood and cardboard. She's also used oil paints, guache, charcoal, pastels, and more.

Liz's true passion is abstract art. She's gone through various "phases", from circles to plant-like designs, and is now working on her "quilts" series. Each quilt is focused on a different color.
